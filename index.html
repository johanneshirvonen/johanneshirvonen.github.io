Johannes Hirvonen

I am a doctoral student at Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität München and research assistant in the project LIDIVOKA at Universität Wien.

In my dissertation I explore how language contact can influence systems of negative indefinite pronouns and adverbs, focussing on the languages of the Russian Volga-Kama-Area.

I have previously worked on a number of topics in Meadow Mari, including Null Subjects, Negative Concord, and Information Structure. Furthermore, I have worked on Null Subjects in Livonian, and the morphophonology of the Estonian short illative or additive.
